    Harvest Apple Grilled Cheese

    seasonal favorite featuring sharp cheddar cheese, thinly sliced tart apples

    a hint of whole grain mustard on buttery, toasted cinnamon-raisin bread. A sweet and savory delight that bridges the taste of fall harvest with cozy comfort

    Golden Autumn Salad

    Embrace the essence of autumn with this vibrant salad

    Combining roasted butternut squash, dried cranberries, spiced pecans, and goat cheese over a bed of mixed greens, all tossed in a maple-cider vinaigrette. It's a harvest celebration in every bite

    Roasted Red Pepper & Tomato Bisque

    Inspired by Spanish cuisine and flavors from South America.

    A velvety, rich bisque combining the sweet and smoky flavors of roasted red peppers and ripe tomatoes, garnished with a dollop of basil-infused cream. This elegant soup brings a burst of color and a touch of sophistication to your meal

    Hearty Lentil & Vegetable Stew

    Perfect for a cold day

    A nourishing and flavorful stew loaded with lentils, carrots, celery, and spinach, simmered in a savory vegetable broth. This comforting blend is a testament to the power of simple ingredients to produce profound flavors, offering warmth and nutrition in every spoonful

    Autumn Bliss Grilled Cheese

    Indulge in a symphony of autumn flavors with our Autumn Bliss Grilled Cheese.

    Featuring layers of aged cheddar and smoked gouda nestled between slices of rustic multigrain bread, it's accented by caramelized apples, crispy bacon, and a hint of rosemary-infused honey. This grilled cheese is a delightful fusion of sweet and savory, capturing the essence of fall in every bite.
